CC Stinson

Austin, Texas

CC Stinson attended high school in Germany which fueled her passion for traveling. She has visited France, Portugal, Spain, Italy, South Korea, Canada, Mexico, and Germany. Out of all the countries, Italia was her favorite! The culture is inviting, the food phenomenal, and the architecture will leave your eyes glazed over by it's beauty.

One of the marvelous wonders in florencia italia is the Duomo. Consisting of two interconnected ogival shells, the cathedral's octagonal dome was erected between 1418 and 1434 to a design which Filippo Brunelleschi entered in a competition in 1418 but which was only accepted, after much controversy, in 1420. A masterpiece capable of withstanding lightning, earthquakes and the passage of time, it continues to enchant all those who observe it from afar. The dome has a diameter of 45.5 metres.
